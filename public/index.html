Reasons to Use QPrompt

QPrompt is a high-quality, feature-rich, teleprompter app made with productivity and smooth performance in mind. It aims to solve the needs of teleprompter operators working in the events industry and of video content creators working individually or in small teams.

QPrompt is free to download and use for any purpose, including commercial use.

It's available for Linux, Windows, and macOS, among other operating systems. Native support for each of these systems helps ensure smooth animations even on low-end hardware.

QPrompt's ability to make its background semi-transparent also make it fit for use in video conference calls and displaying apps with other video streams in the background.

QPrompt's adjustable reading area helps ensure people's eyes face the camera instead of darting left and right, regardless of whether they're recording from a webcam or a studio teleprompter.

QPrompt's rich text editor allows you to open and copy-paste formatted text from popular office suites. If you install LibreOffice on your computer, QPrompt will leverage it in the background to open a wide variety of formats.

Readers with dislexia can greatly benefit from QPrompt thanks to the horizontal bars that highlight the reading area, as well as the inclusion of the OpenDyslexic font.

A big thanks to ___ for pointing us in this direction.

Make changes on the fly by swiftly entering and leaving prompt mode, or by double clicking where you wish to edit while prompting. Press ESC or double click again to exit edit while prompting mode.

Navigate quickly through your script with customizable keyboard shortcuts. These inputs can be mapped to match that of other devices that act as keyboard emulators, such as presentation clickers and Contour Shuttle Controllers.

Use in combination with KDE Connect's Presentation Mode to control the prompter's velocity through your phone.

Named and nameless markers allow you to instantly jump to any predetermined location on the script. Assign a key-combo from your keyboard, pressing said keys while prompting will jump to the desired location.

Built-in chronometer let's you time your scripts and computes the time to reach the end of the scripts.

While nothing beats dedicated hardware for screen duplication, QPrompt let's you prompt to multiple screens connected to your computer, through a feature called Screen Projections.

For dedicated hardware for screen duplication, we recommend Decimator. We are not sponsored by them, but people in our community loves their products.

Thanks to a worldwide community, QPrompt is available in many of the world's most commonly spoken languages. *QPrompt also comes with fonts that allow prompting text in writing systems of over 180 languages. RTL writing systems are also officially supported.

*The full set of fonts, access to system fonts, and RTL support is only available on the versions that you download to your computer, not on the version for web browsers.

Due to the lack of support for the MOS and NDI protocol, QPrompt is not recommended for use in traditional newsroom environments. If you're interested in supporting the development of these features, please reach out.
